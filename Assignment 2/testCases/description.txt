TEST_CASE_1 - Sign Up
Preconditions:
1. Open browser
2. Insert to the browser address bar the following link: https://www.demoblaze.com/

Steps:
1. Click to the "Sign up" link in the top right corner of the page
2. Insert your username and password to the corresponding fields of the modal window
3. Click to the "Sign up" button to confirm registration





TEST_CASE_2 - Log In
Preconditions:
1. Open browser
2. Insert to the browser address bar the following link: https://www.demoblaze.com/

Steps:
1. Click to the "Log in" link in the top right corner of the page
2. Insert your username and password to the corresponding fields of the modal window
3. Click to the "Log in" button to confirm registration





TEST_CASE_3 - Log Out
Preconditions:
1. Open browser
2. Insert to the browser address bar the following link: https://www.demoblaze.com/
3. Login to your account (See TEST_CASE_2)

Steps:
1. Click to the "Log out" link in the top right corner of the page





TEST_CASE_4 - Add item to the cart
Preconditions:
1. Open browser
2. Insert to the browser address bar the following link: https://www.demoblaze.com/
3. Login to your account (See TEST_CASE_2)

Steps:
1. Choose a category of items in the menu on the left side of the page
2. Click to the one item, for example "Nokia lumia 1520"
3. Click to the "Add to cart" button





TEST_CASE_5 - Make an order and payment
Preconditions:
1. Open browser
2. Insert to the browser address bar the following link: https://www.demoblaze.com/
3. Login to your account (See TEST_CASE_2)

Steps:
1. Click to the "Cart" link in the top center corner of the page
2. Click to the "Place order" button
3. Insert data for order (your name, country, city and credit card data) to the corresponding field of the modal window
4. Click to "Purchase" button
